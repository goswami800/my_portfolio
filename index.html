DevOps Principles I Live By

Core philosophies that guide my approach to building and maintaining robust systems:

Automation First

Everything that can be automated, should be automated. From infrastructure provisioning to testing and deployments, reducing manual intervention minimizes errors and accelerates delivery.

Security Integration

Security is not an afterthought but integrated into every phase of the development lifecycle (DevSecOps). Vulnerability scanning, secret management, and compliance checks are built into pipelines.

Observability & Monitoring

Systems should be observable with comprehensive logging, metrics, and tracing. Proactive monitoring with actionable alerts ensures rapid incident response and system reliability.

Continuous Improvement

Embrace a culture of experimentation, learning from failures, and iterating rapidly. Regular retrospectives and post-incident reviews drive continuous improvement in processes and systems.
